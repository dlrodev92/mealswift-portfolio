Design Rationale

Key Decisions

🎨

Colour Psychology

Ocean Blue (#288EC1) communicates trust and reliability. Lime Yellow (#E1ED30) stimulates appetite and creates energy without replicating competitors like Deliveroo (teal) or Uber Eats (green/black). Deliberate differentiation through colour.

📱

Mobile-First Responsive

All pages designed at 375px mobile first, then scaled to 1440px desktop. Directly addresses MealSwift's non-responsive layout causing drop-offs on mobile devices. WCAG 2.1 AA compliant.

🧠

Nielsen's Heuristics

Navigation redesign applies Visibility of System Status and User Control principles. Real-time order tracking (page 5) resolves the core post-order anxiety identified in the problem analysis.

👁️

Gestalt Principles

Proximity and similarity group restaurant cards and menu items into scannable clusters. Consistent visual hierarchy across all 5 pages reduces cognitive load and supports quick decision-making.

🛒

Conversion Optimisation

Sticky cart panel, trust badges, and a prominent Place Order CTA address abandoned cart issues. The two-column checkout layout minimises friction at the most critical conversion point.

♿

Accessibility (WCAG 2.1)

Colour contrast ratios verified for AA compliance. Cascadia Mono chosen for legibility. Clear focus states, descriptive labels, and logical tab order ensure inclusive access for all users.
